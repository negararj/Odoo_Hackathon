# CSR and Sustainability (Odoo Module)

A module for managing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and Sustainability initiatives.

## Employees:
1. They have some skills (inherited from odoo employee model) 
2. They can choose projects from the defined projects by NGOs and geting sustainability_point by accomplishing those projects. 
3. They can exchange the sustainability_point to do some activities like planting trees, donating and etc. and by each purchase day will get money with defined currency (O2)
- we will have a leaderboard of employees and I want it to be sorted according to O2. 
4. The employees can see their projects (pending and done) as it is already implemented in odoo model

## NGOs: 
1. They are portal users. 
2. I want to inherit the portal user of the odoo and costumize it to let them add projects and activities and see the projects and activities that are already done or pending. 

## Install
1. Copy `csr_sustainability/` into your addons path.
2. Update apps list and install **CSR and Sustainability**.
